Dr. HITESHI TANDON

Assistant Professorr Google Scholar | ORCID | WebofScience | Scopus | ResearchGate

Dr. Hiteshi Tandon is an Assistant Professor at P P Savani University, India with a rich background in Theoretical and Computational Chemistry. A former postdoctoral researcher at IISc Bangalore and DST Woman Scientist-C, she brings a wealth of knowledge and innovation to her work. With a Ph.D. from Manipal University Jaipur and Masters from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (NIT) Jaipur, Dr. Tandon explores the fascinating world of Density Functional Theory and Chemical Reactivity Descriptors. She’s published over 30 papers, contributed to key conferences, and even shared her expertise as a speaker on intellectual property rights. When she's not pushing the boundaries of Chemistry, Dr. Tandon enjoys guiding students through their research journeys. Connect with her to dive deeper into the molecular universe!

AASHKA TALATI

Student Of Chemistry B.Sc In Organic Chemistry

Aashka Talati is an MSc Chemistry student at P P Savani University, where she benefits from excellent faculty guidance. With a strong interest in computational chemistry and a passion for coding, Aashka is dedicated to creating 3D molecular formations to enhance understanding of chemical processes. She is currently developing a website aimed at helping the new generation visualize complex concepts, making learning efficient and engaging. Known for her ability to explain her work clearly, Aashka is committed to fostering a deeper appreciation for the intricacies of chemistry.
